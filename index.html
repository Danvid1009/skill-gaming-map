How to Use This Map

Hover over any state or province to see detailed skill gaming information including capacity score, permitted types, and important regulatory notes.

Zoom & Pan: Use mouse wheel to zoom, click and drag to pan, or use the +/− buttons. Click ⌂ to reset view.

Color coding: Red indicates restrictions (score 0), progressing through yellow/green to blue for most permissive (score 5).
